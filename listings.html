Nemesis

Nemesis is a semi-cooperative science fiction board game for 1-5 players, set on the spaceship Nemesis. Players, as crew members, explore the ship and deal with an alien threat while suffering from temporary amnesia. The game combines cooperation and confrontational mechanisms, and to win, players must survive, reach Earth, and achieve their chosen objectives.
